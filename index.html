Delivery Pace — did we deliver enough check-ins?
Budget = (this month's seasonal target − consumed so far this month) ÷ days remaining × 7
Deficit or surplus rolls forward within the month. Resets each month to its own seasonal target.

Pace > 1.0x = ahead of where you need to be to land the month.
Demand Pace — are we filling the annual pipeline?
Budget = (€13.30M annual target − YTD consumed) ÷ weeks remaining in year
Accounts for seasonality automatically. Compares new bookings made this week against what's needed to hit the full year.

Pace > 1.0x = booking faster than needed to hit €13.30M annual goal.
